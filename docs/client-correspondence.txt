HullSafe.co.uk: Full Strategic Proposal (2026) v1
1. The "Quad-Safe" Brand Identity
Safe for the Hull: Preserving anti-foul with precision ROV nylon brushes.
Safe for the Sea: Eco-compliant debris capture (MDL Marina approved).
Safe for the Wallet: Avoiding the £500+ cost of a marina lift-out.
Safe for the Crew: 4K early-warning visual reports on vitals like hull integrity, intakes, and anodes.

2. The Website Architecture (The "Active" Hub & Spoke Model)
Each Hub is a primary landing page designed to rank for broad, high-volume keywords. These pages host the "Spokes," which are deep-dive sub-pages for specific search terms.
Hub 1: Visual Hull Inspections (The "See" Pillar)
The Hub Page: Ranks for "Underwater Hull Inspection" and "Boat Survey South Devon."
Spoke 1: 4K Underwater Video Scans – Delivering high-def footage for owner review.
Spoke 2: Pre-Season & Pre-Sale Condition Checks – Documenting waterline status for buyers/sellers.
Spoke 3: Anode & Intake Monitoring – Specific visual checks on the "vitals" that prevent breakdowns.
Hub 2: Robotic Hull Maintenance (The "Clean" Pillar)
The Hub Page: Ranks for "In-Water Boat Cleaning" and "Mobile Hull Scrubbing."
Spoke 1: In-Water Hull Scrubbing – The core Keelcrab service for 10-24m yachts.
Spoke 2: Eco-Friendly Bio-fouling Removal – Highlighting debris capture technology for marina compliance.
Spoke 3: Performance (Hull & Propeller Cleaning) – Focused on speed and fuel efficiency for leisure craft.
Hub 3: Fleet & Subscription Services (The "Stability" Pillar)
The Hub Page: Ranks for "Boat Maintenance Plans" and "Yacht Management Devon."
Spoke 1: Sailing Schools – Bespoke plans to keep training vessels operational 24/7.
Spoke 2: Charter Fleets – Pre-charter "clean and check" services to ensure client satisfaction.
Spoke 3: Recurring Membership Plans – Our tiered subscription model for private owners.

3. Regional SEO & Expansion Roadmap
We will establish a powerhouse brand identity while creating location-specific "entry points" for expansion.
Homepage (Brixham Base): hullsafe.co.uk
Targets "Brixham Marina" and establishes the high-tech brand authority.
Torquay Expansion Page: hullsafe.co.uk/locations/torquay-marina
Unique content focused on the luxury motor-yacht community and MDL standards.
Dartmouth Expansion Page: hullsafe.co.uk/locations/dartmouth-river-dart
Unique content focused on the specific silt and algae challenges of river-moored yachts.

4. The "Stability" Upsell Logic
The website is designed to convert "transactional" customers into "recurring" members.
The Hook: "One-off cleans solve a problem; a membership prevents one."
The Offer: Members receive priority scheduling in peak season and a discounted "per-clean" rate. This provides the operator with stable, predictable monthly recurring revenue (MRR).

5. Legal & Operational Safeguards
Terminology: All "Inspection" keywords are framed as "Visual Condition Reporting" to maintain SEO while protecting the business from surveyor-level liability.
The "HullSafe Report": A digital delivery (Video link + PDF checklist) documenting what the ROV saw. The final evaluation remains in the owner's hands.
Operator Focus: The operator is positioned as a "Robotic Technician," focusing on the precision of the Keelcrab technology.

Summary of the Strategy
By deploying this 3-Hub / 9-Spoke model, we are "blanketing" the search results for our target market (10-24m yachts, schools, and charters) in South Devon. We capture the high-intent keywords (Cleaning) and the research-phase keywords (Inspections) and funnel them into a stable, subscription-based business model.
